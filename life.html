Meet Your Naturalist

Our experienced naturalist guides are passionate conservationists who have lived alongside this ecosystem for years. They bring the forest to life — pointing out hidden wildlife, ancient trees, and the delicate balance that keeps this rainforest thriving.

All tours are led by a certified guide. Group size is kept small (max 8 guests) to ensure a personal, immersive experience with minimal environmental impact.
