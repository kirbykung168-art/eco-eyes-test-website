Our Philosophy

Food as
a Forest Story

Every dish at Eco Café is a celebration of Thai agricultural heritage. We source from local farmers, forage from our own forest gardens, and prepare food with minimal waste and maximum flavour.

Dining here is not just eating — it's an act of connection. Between you, the land, and the people who nurture it. Our Eco Coffee is grown sustainably and brewed in-house; every cup comes with a seedling to take home.

The Eco Coffee Story

Every Cup Plants
a Tree

Our Eco Coffee is sourced from sustainable highland farms in Northern Thailand. Cold-brewed in-house for 18 hours, it delivers a smooth, rich flavour with low acidity — unlike anything you'll find in the city.

Every coffee purchased includes a seedling for you to take home and plant. A small ritual that turns every sip into an act of reforestation.
