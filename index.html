Our Story

Where Barren Land
Became Forest

Eco Eyes Village is a private nature conservatory and learning centre in Nakhon Nayok, Thailand. We protect more than 30 Rai of land transformed from barren farmland into thriving forest through years of dedicated reforestation.

Today, more than 150 plant species and 300 species of birds and animals call Eco Eyes home — a living testament to what nature can do when given the chance to heal. Now open for glamping, guided nature tours, and mindfulness retreats.

Our Mission

Raising Awareness,
One Forest at a Time

Glaciers are melting, sea levels are rising, and wildlife is scrambling to keep pace. At Eco Eyes Village, we aim to educate this generation — and the next — about the threats that unsustainable development poses to our shared future.

Our goal is to be a role model for sustainable development — passing knowledge of sustainable agriculture to children and adults alike, showing what each of us can do.
