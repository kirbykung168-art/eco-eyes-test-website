Our Mission

A Role Model for
Sustainable Development

Eco Eyes Village began as a single vision: to transform barren farmland into a living forest and prove that sustainable development is not only possible, but essential.

Today, that vision has become a thriving ecosystem — a sanctuary for over 300 species of wildlife and a classroom for thousands of visitors. We believe that protecting the environment and developing our communities are not competing goals, but two sides of the same mission.

Our CSR programme partners with schools, corporations, and community organisations to plant trees, build environmental awareness, and share the knowledge that sustainable living is within everyone's reach.

Join Our Mission

The Name Behind the Mission

'Eyes'
in Eco Eyes Village

The word 'Eyes' in our name carries a meaning far deeper than sight. From the very beginning, Eco Eyes Village has been committed to a mission of education — not just about the natural world, but about expanding access to it for everyone. One of the earliest and most personal inspirations behind our work was our dedication to helping educate blind and visually impaired children.

We believe that nature belongs to everyone — including those who experience the world differently. Through multi-sensory forest experiences, tactile education programmes, and partnerships with schools for the visually impaired, Eco Eyes Village has worked to ensure that the wonders of the natural world are never beyond reach. The smell of rain on soil, the rustle of leaves, the calls of birds — these are gifts that sight cannot contain.

'Eyes' in our name is a reminder: that to truly see the world, you must first open your heart to it. Our work with blind children taught us that vision comes in many forms — and that the forest has something to teach all of us.

What We Do

Our CSR Pillars

Reforestation

Plant a Tree

Since 2015, Eco Eyes Village has planted over 10,000 trees on its property and in surrounding communities. We offer tree-planting experiences for corporate groups, schools, and families who want to make a lasting contribution.

Education

Nature Classroom

Our education programme has welcomed over 5,000 students from schools across Thailand. We teach environmental science, sustainable agriculture, and conservation through hands-on, immersive learning in the forest.

Community

Local Partnerships

We collaborate with local farmers, suppliers, and community organisations to create a sustainable economy around conservation. When the forest thrives, the community thrives — that is the Eco Eyes model.
